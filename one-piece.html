Monkey D. Luffy

Monkey D. Luffy, commonly known as "Straw Hat", is the founder, captain, and strongest combatant of the increasingly infamous and powerful Straw Hat Pirates. He fearlessly pursues the legendary treasure of the late Gol D. Roger in order to become the new Pirate King

Roronoa Zoro

Roronoa Zoro, also known as "Pirate Hunter" Zoro, is publicly recognized as the right-hand man and number two of his crew's captain Monkey D. Luffy. Formerly a bounty hunter, he is the second member of Luffy's crew and the first to join it.
